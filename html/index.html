Posture

Body language

Tracks shoulder alignment, slouching, and fidgeting in real time using on-device pose tracking - nothing is sent anywhere just to watch you.

Voice

Pace & delivery

Measures words per minute, pause length, and filler words like "um" and "like" as you speak, not after the fact.

Content

What you actually said

Follow-up questions adapt to your last answer, and every response gets scored on structure, clarity, and relevance.

How a session works

01

Turn on your camera and mic

Everything runs locally in your browser. Nothing is recorded or stored on a server.

02

Answer out load, like a real interview

Aptitude asks a question, tracks your posture and pace while you answer, then follows up based on what you said.

03

Get a breakdown, not just a grade

Your report shows exactly where you slowed down, slouched, or drifted off-topic, with specific tips to fix it.
